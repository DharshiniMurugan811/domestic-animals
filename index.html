About Domestic Animals

Domestic animals are tamed and raised by humans for food, work, protection, and companionship. They play an important role in our lives.


  • Dogs are loyal and friendship animals that prvides security and assistance to humans.
  • Cats are Friendly pet animal that helps control rats and playful an affectionate nature.
  • Cows are valuable farm animals primarily raised for milk and dairy production.
  • Goats are hardy farm raised for milk, meat, and breeding purposes.
  • Sheeps are farm animals valued for wool, meat, and milk production.
  • Hens an Roosters are poultry birds commonly raised for eggs, chicks and breeding.
  • Ducks are waterflow raised for eggs, meat, an breeding.
  • Turkeys are large poultry birds raised for mainly meat, egg production and also breeding.
